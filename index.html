Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed. Add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture if desired.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: When the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to coat evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the edges set but the middle is slightly runny, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette over the fillings, let it cook for a minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with extra salt and pepper if needed.
